Student Mental Health & Well-being Survey

Conducted by: Society for Mental Welfare, UAF

Purpose: Understand student mental health needs and improve support services

About This Survey

This survey is designed to help us understand the mental health needs of UAF students. Your participation is completely voluntary and anonymous. The survey includes standardized assessment tools (GAD-7, PHQ-9, PSS-10) that help measure anxiety, depression, and stress levels.

Please note: If you are experiencing thoughts of self-harm, please reach out to the UAF Counseling Center or a trusted individual immediately. Help is available.

Informed Consent

By proceeding with this survey, you confirm that:

  • You are participating voluntarily
  • You understand your responses are anonymous
  • You are at least 18 years old
You must provide consent to continue with the survey
